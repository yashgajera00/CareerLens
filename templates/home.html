HOW IT WORKS
About CareerLens

CareerLens is a smart career guidance and skill analysis platform designed to help students make informed career decisions. Instead of providing generic suggestions, this application evaluates users based on their interests, job preferences, and actual skill levels.

Career-Oriented Skill Assessment

After login, users select their preferred career domain or job role. Based on this selection, the system presents skill-specific multiple-choice questions that evaluate relevant abilities rather than general knowledge.

Skill Analysis & Career Insights

The application analyzes user responses to identify strengths, weaknesses, and overall skill readiness. A detailed career report is generated, including suitability scores, improvement areas, and alternative career options where applicable.

Learning & Skill Improvement Support

CareerLens also supports continuous learning by recommending curated learning resources and videos. These resources help users improve weak skill areas and prepare effectively for their desired career path.
